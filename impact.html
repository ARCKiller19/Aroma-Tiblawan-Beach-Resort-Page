Our Region

Governor Generoso, Davao Oriental

Davao Oriental is one of Mindanao's most scenic and culturally rich provinces — home to indigenous Mandaya communities, pristine coastlines, and warm, resilient people. Aroma Tiblawan was built to celebrate this land and share it responsibly with visitors from across the Davao Region and beyond.

Governor Generoso remains one of the province's lesser-known gems — and we intend to keep it that way. Growth must be gentle, intentional, and always centered on the well-being of its people.

We will never grow at the expense of our neighbors, our coastline, or our culture. Every guest is a partner in sustainable coastal tourism. We thank you for choosing Aroma Tiblawan — not just as a destination, but as a cause.
